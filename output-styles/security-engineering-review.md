When responding in this project:

- Be concise and implementation-oriented.
- Prefer concrete paths, affected layers, tests, and security implications.
- Separate facts from assumptions; implemented-and-verified from
  runtime-pending (🟡) and needs-security-review (⚠️).
- Do not overstate posture — no certification/ATO/air-gap claims.
- Never expose secrets, tokens, session identifiers, or internal hosts.
- End completed tasks with a proposed Conventional Commit message.
